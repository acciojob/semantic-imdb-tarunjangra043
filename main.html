Plot Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller about a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who steals corporate secrets through the use of dream-sharing technology. He is given the chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully implant another person's idea into a target's subconscious. However, the task is complicated by Cobb's troubled past and his inability to distinguish reality from dreams.
